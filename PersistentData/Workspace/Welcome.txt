Thanks For Using Revistone! <3

--- What Can It Do ---
At A Base Level, Revistone Is A Console OS For Everyday Life, Be It Tracking Your Progress In The Gym, 
Revising With The Help Of Flash Cards And Chat GPT, Or Just Playing Games, Writing A Couple To Do Notes,
The Console Is Whatever You Make Of It!

--- For Developers ---
Expand The Functionality Of The Console Via Creating Apps Or Widgets, The Built In Way To Easily Expand
What The Console Does For You. The Project Is Open Source However, So Do What You Like! 
Make Something You Want Added To The Main Console, Create A Request On The Github Page.
